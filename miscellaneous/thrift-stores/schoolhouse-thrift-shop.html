Off Charlottesville Rio Road, 5 minutes from Downtown. Three rooms full of gently used clothing, accessories, home goods, books, CDs/DVDs/records, video games, baby equipment, small electronis, and more. Open six days a week (except Sunday), 10am-4pm. I got Sony PSP hand-held video console with 10 games for $5. Support the locals or spend 50 times more here.

The Schoolhouse Thrift Store opened in its current location, a repurposed county school building, in 1992. All items are accepted on a donation basis only. The shop is staffed primarily by community members on a volunteer basis.

The main purpose has always been to raise funds for local causes and charities. Portion of donations are provided to families in need through local service agencies.
