Another beautiful crushed gravel country road that travels up to 8 miles in one direction. Take 250 West to Miller School Road and follow for about 2.5 miles until you see Dick Woods Road on your right. The parking area is kindly donated and maintained by our friends at the Miller School of Albemarle.

Recommended Gear:

Isolated Water Bottle, Cycling Backpack, First Aid Kit.

Recommended Meetups and Other Groups:

Charlottesville Area Mountain Bike Club

Recommended Books and Videos:
