Stinson Vineyards: Music, Grilling and Sunset!

Address: 4744 Sugar Hollow Rd, Crozet, VA 22932Map

Coordinates: 38.0744586,-78.6388308 Map

Parking: At the VineyardsMap

Distance: 15 miles from Charlottesville

Food and Drinks: Bring picnic and buy wine!Map

Stinson Vineyards hosts the popular live music and fires up grills from May to October making it a perfect place for late afternoon picnic. You just have to bring your own meat, seafood or veggies, spices, condiments and picnic supplies are provided free - get a glass or a bottle of Stinson Wines, relax in an adirondack chair and enjoy the vies of sunset over the Blue Ridge Mountains. Check out their facebook page to stay up-to-date on about performing musicians and (inclement) weather cancellations. And the vineyard grounds are kid and pet friendly!
