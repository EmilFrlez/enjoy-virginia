New student or employee at UVA and wanting to know more about the place than the man? Is Monday night a night free to be able to hear what the buzz is about? Here's your chance for what may be my last UVA tour for 2008 as the nights get short. I can make the group larger if the interest is there. Essentially the tour covers Jefferson's architectural interest and background, the principles behind the design for the Lawn, pointing out some architectural features, and concluding how the Lawn is a representational model for architecture across the United States. The Lawn is an architectural gem - architects from around the world go there to see it, and many of them write about it. For those who do not know me, for conversational puposes I am an architect here in town. I did not go to UVA, so you will get an unbiased "Hokie" presentation more in keeping with how architects interpret the design based on their own observations and background. No major walking, just the distance the Lawn covers and its terrain. This is an alcohol-free event. Please do not bring or buy any alcohol.
