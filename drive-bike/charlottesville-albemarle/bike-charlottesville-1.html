Charlottesville Neigbourhoods Bike Ride

Departure Address: Charlottesville PavillionMap

Start Coordinates: 38.0294685, -78.4776788Map

Parking: 7th St NE and Maple StMap

Total Distance: 5-10 miles

Food and Drinks: Downtown Charlottesville RestaurantsMap

Let cruise around some downtown Charlottesville neighborhoods on bicycle! You can start out riding around some of the beautiful neighborhoods in northern downtown and then dabble into some parts of Belmont. It is quite hilly in some places. The riding pace should be casual enough so you can see all that there is to see, but with enough rigor you should be able to pedal up hills – not walk up ‘em.

Good starting point is the Sprint Pavilion on the east end of the Downtown.

You can return back to the Sprint Pavilion to put your bike back on your car, if needed, and then proceed to the sequential dinner adventure afterwards (if desired, see maps).

Bring water, your own bike, helmet (required, I have a couple of extras), jacket (just in case it is a cool evening) and bike lock (if you want to lock your bike for the sequential dinner adventure afterwards). Non-cotton clothing is preferable.

Recommended Gear:

Isolated Water Bottle, Cycling Backpack, First Aid Kit.

Meetups and Other Groups:

Charlottesville Bicycle Club

Recommended Books:

Falkon Road Biking Virginia, A Guide to Road Cycling in Central Virginia, Backroad Bicycling in the Blue Ridge.
