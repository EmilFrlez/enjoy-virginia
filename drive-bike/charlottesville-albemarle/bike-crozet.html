Road Bike Ride in Crozet

Departure Address: 1110 Old Trail Dr, Crozet, VA 22932Map

Start Coordinates: 38.058383, -78.7104928Map

Parking: Grit Coffee Bar & CafeMap

Total Distance: 10 milesMap

Food and Drinks: Grit Coffee, King Family Vineyard Picnic or Pro Renata Brewery

Explore Crozet and neighbouring countryside on a bike (road, hybrid or mountain bikes OK). You could do 1-2 hour loop (10-20 miles) on the (mostly) flat roads. Depending on your preferences afterwards you might stop by (1) Starr Hill Brewery for a tour and/or beer tasting, (2) watch a Polo Match and do wine tasting at the King's Family Vineyards, or just stop for a tea/coffee break at the Grit coffee shop.

Recommended loop: north on Old Trail Dr, left on Jarman's Gap Rd, right on Lanetown Rd (VA Rt. 684), left on Mint Spring Rd to the Mint Springs Park and back.

Recommended Gear:

Isolated Water Bottle, Cycling Backpack, First Aid Kit.

Meetups and Other Groups:

Charlottesville Bicycle Club

Recommended Books:

Falkon Road Biking Virginia, A Guide to Road Cycling in Central Virginia, Backroad Bicycling in the Blue Ridge.
