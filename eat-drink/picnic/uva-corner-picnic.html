Picnic at UVa Corner

Address: University Way, Charlottesville, VA 22904Map

Coordinates: 38.0415262,-78.5023957 Map

Parking: Map

Food and Drinks: The UVa Corner Restaurants and Delis

The U.Va. Corner features an eclectic mix of restaurants and shops both old and new. Whether you are just visiting for the day, a student needing a break, or a local from the area, the lush and verdant spot directly across from the corner is a perfect place to enjoy all the Corner has to offer.

Bring your own picnic or carry out something you’ve purchased from one of the many Corner restaurants, including Little John’s, Mellow Mushroom, and xxxxx. Find a perfect tree to sit under, and enjoy people watching as you spread out on this ample picnic blanket. You may also enjoy perusing this book on trees where you’ll surely identify some of the many surrounding you. Or, if history is your thing, you could learn a little bit about the University from this book while enjoying the warm and gentle breezes finding their way to you.

If you’d rather be active after enjoying your meal, consider stopping by the time-tested supplier of all goods U.Va: Mincer’s. Here you can find U.Va. sports balls, frisbees, and any and all U.Va. emblem clothing or gear you might desire.

Whatever you decide, the U.Va. Corner is a fantastic spot to soak in and savor the essence of U.Va.
