Big Levels Jeep trail is in the George Washington National Forest. A great day trip for local Jeerpers to enjoy nature and get away from the City for a little while...

Directions: From I-64: take exit 96 (Waynesboro/Lyndhurst exit) onto Route 664. Follow toward Sherando Lake Recreation Area. Turn right onto the Coal Road (Forest Service Road 42) before you get to Sherando Lake Recreation Area. Follow the Coal Road for approximately 7 miles to Forest Service Road 162 on the left. Turn left onto FDR 162. This is the start of the Stony Run Jeep Trail and will take you to the Blue Ridge Parkway (approximately 12 miles long). Seasonal road closure due to weather and road conditions to protect the natural resources. Note: This road gets rougher the farther up you go. This is definitely a 4-wheel drive area only.
