The acac Waterpark offers both fun in the sun and relaxation in the shade. We also offer the area’s longest outdoor pool season!

Adventure Central Waterpark features:

  • Olympic-sized pool with lap lanes, diving board, and basketball hoop,
  • Beach-entry pool,
  • Two waterslides with 2 twisting slides,
  • Kiddie pool with spray garden,
  • Basketball court,
  • Kid game area – New 2017!,
  • Shaded and full-sun lounging areas,
  • Covered pavilion (great for parties!),
  • Poolside Café offering pizza, sandwiches, salads, smoothies, craft beers, and more!
