Silverback Destillery is (at the moment ;8-) only destillery located on Rt 151 that transveres the Rockfish Valley.

Its offerings include Beringei Vodka, Strange Monkey Gin, Blackback Lucky 13 Rye Whiskey and Honey Rye. They offer offer visitors a menu of straight pours and mixer cocktail drinks to taste the lineup of their spirits.

It is worth mentioning that their tasting room is pet friendly! They just ask that your dog is on leash.
