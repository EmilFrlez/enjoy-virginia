Go to the Preddy Creek and hit the trails for a couple of hours. The mountain bike trails are suitable for all levels beginner thru advanced. This is a no drop ride....the slowest rider will determine our pace. For this ride you will need to bring your own bike with tires that are suitable for dirt track riding, a helmet, and a water bottle. Then hit the dirt and enjoy this exciting sport.

Recommended Gear:

Isolated Water Bottle, Cycling Backpack, First Aid Kit.

Recommended Meetups and Other Groups:

Charlottesville Area Mountain Bike Club

Recommended Books:

Falcon Mountain Bike Virginia, Mountain Bike! Virginia, Backroad Bicycling in the Blue Ridge.
