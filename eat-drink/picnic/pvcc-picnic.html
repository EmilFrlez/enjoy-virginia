There’s a well-kept secret picnic spot in Charlottesville: The PVCC Campus. Covering such a large area, this campus has many spots featuring spectacular views of the Blue Ridge. Catch the sunset while sipping on your favorite bubbly in the comfort of this portable lounge chair.

The campus has ample parking so you can bring whatever you need. Consider trying your hand at some artwork as the options for beautiful scenery are endless. Whether you decide to picnic by the lake, near the hiking trails, or up on the hill looking towards the mountains you will enjoy exquisite views.

PVCC is also a perfect spot to capture high quality moments with this sporty camera. Why not add to the views by really decking out your picnic! These items allow you to really glam it up without breaking the bank.

And don’t worry about keeping the secret: PVCC has so many spots for eclectic picnics, there’s plenty of space to go around. Enjoy!
